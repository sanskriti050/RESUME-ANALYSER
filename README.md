# 📄 ResumeLens AI – Career Intelligence Platform

> **Transform your resume into a recruiter-ready profile with AI-powered career insights.**

ResumeLens AI is an AI-powered Career Intelligence Platform that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), identify skill gaps, tailor resumes to specific job descriptions, and receive personalized AI-driven recommendations to improve their chances of landing interviews.

Unlike traditional resume checkers, ResumeLens AI combines ATS analysis, job description matching, skill gap detection, resume enhancement, and career guidance into one intelligent platform.

## 🚀 Features

- 📄 Upload Resume (PDF)
- 🤖 AI-Powered Resume Analysis
- 📊 ATS Compatibility Score

- ✨ AI Resume Improvement Suggestions
- 📝 Resume Summary Generation
- 💼 Recruiter-Oriented Feedback
- 📈 Resume Strength Analysis
- 📥 Download Professional Analysis Report

---

## 💡 Why ResumeLens AI?

Recruiters spend only a few seconds reviewing each resume, and most companies rely on Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter candidates before a human ever sees their application.

ResumeLens AI helps candidates:

- Improve ATS compatibility
- Tailor resumes for specific job roles
- Identify missing technical and soft skills
- Strengthen resume content
- Increase interview opportunities
